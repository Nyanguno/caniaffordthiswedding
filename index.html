Frequently Asked Questions

How much should I budget for my wedding?

Financial experts recommend spending 10-15% of your annual income on your wedding. However, the right budget depends on your savings, timeline, and priorities. Our calculator helps you find a realistic budget based on your specific situation.

What factors affect wedding costs the most?

Guest count is the biggest factor affecting wedding costs, as it impacts catering, venue size, invitations, and favors. Location, season, day of the week, and level of formality also significantly influence costs.

How can I reduce my wedding costs?

The most effective ways to reduce wedding costs include: reducing guest count, choosing off-peak dates (weekdays or off-season), selecting less expensive venues, DIY decorations, buffet-style dining, and digital invitations.

How far in advance should I start saving for my wedding?

Most couples benefit from 12-18 months of saving time. This allows you to save gradually without straining your budget and gives you flexibility to book preferred vendors and venues.

Are the cost estimates accurate for my location?

Our calculator uses regional multipliers based on extensive market research. While individual vendor prices vary, our estimates provide a solid foundation for budget planning. Always get local quotes for the most accurate pricing.
